<p><p>The metalized ceramic substrates market comprises various methods for enhancing substrate conductivity. The Molybdenum and Manganese Method involves applying metal layers for thermal and electrical performance. Gold Plating Method uses gold to achieve excellent conductivity and corrosion resistance, ideal for high-performance applications. Copper Plating Method offers cost-effectiveness and strong conductivity but may require additional protection against oxidation. The Tin Plating Method provides a good balance of conductivity and solderability, commonly used in electronics assembly. Each method caters to specific application needs in electronics.</p></p>
