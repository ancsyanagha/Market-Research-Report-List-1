<p><p>The nitrogen gas booster market is primarily categorized into single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed to compress nitrogen in a single cycle, making them suitable for low-pressure applications with moderate flow rates. In contrast, multi-stage pumps utilize multiple compression stages to achieve higher pressures and flow rates, catering to industries requiring robust performance, such as aerospace and oil and gas. Both types serve critical roles in efficient nitrogen delivery, enhancing operational efficiency across various applications.</p></p>
