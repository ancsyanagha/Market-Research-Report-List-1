<p><strong>The Metallic Effect Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic effect paint market is categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic paints typically contain higher levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), providing a durable finish and better adhesion on various surfaces. They are favored for demanding applications but require careful handling. In contrast, water-based metallic paints offer lower VOC levels, making them more environmentally friendly and easier to clean. However, they may have a longer drying time and slightly less durability compared to their solvent-based counterparts.</p></p>
