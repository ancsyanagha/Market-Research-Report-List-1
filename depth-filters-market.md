<p><strong>The Depth Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom-In–Top-Out Mode (Inline Configuration)</li><li>Bottom-In–Bottom-Out Mode (T-Configuration)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Depth filters are designed for efficient particle removal in various liquid filtration applications. In the Bottom-In–Top-Out mode (Inline Configuration), the liquid enters the filter from the bottom and exits from the top, promoting effective retention of particulates while minimizing pressure loss. Conversely, the Bottom-In–Bottom-Out mode (T-Configuration) allows the liquid to flow from the bottom and exit through a side outlet, enabling easier maintenance and higher capacity for solid loading. Both modes cater to specific operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
