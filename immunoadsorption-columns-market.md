<p><p>The immunoadsorption columns market is primarily segmented into two types: tryptophan immunoadsorption columns and protein A immunoadsorption columns. Tryptophan columns are designed to selectively remove specific antibodies using tryptophan-binding techniques, making them suitable for targeted therapies. Protein A columns utilize high-affinity binding to immunoglobulin G (IgG) antibodies, facilitating the purification and removal of antibodies in research and clinical applications. Both types play crucial roles in biopharmaceutical development, diagnostics, and therapeutic processes within the biomedical field.</p></p>
