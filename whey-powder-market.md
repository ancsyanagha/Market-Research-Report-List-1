<p><p>The whey powder market comprises primarily two types: concentrate and isolate forms. Whey protein concentrate contains about 70-80% protein, retaining more nutrients and fats, making it ideal for general health and nutritional supplements. In contrast, whey protein isolate boasts a higher protein content, around 90%, with minimal fats and lactose, catering to athletes and fitness enthusiasts seeking pure protein sources. Both forms serve diverse applications in food, beverages, and supplements, driving significant demand in health-conscious markets.</p></p>
