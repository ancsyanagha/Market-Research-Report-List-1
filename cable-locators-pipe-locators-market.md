<p><strong>The Cable Locators & Pipe Locators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Location</li><li>Passive Location</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cable and pipe locators are essential tools for underground infrastructure detection. The market can be categorized into two main types: active and passive location. Active location systems use a transmitter to send signals through cables or pipes, allowing for precise tracking. In contrast, passive location systems detect existing signals or electromagnetic fields emitted by utilities without transmitting additional signals. Each type serves specific applications, with active locators typically offering greater accuracy, while passive locators provide a more straightforward, non-intrusive approach.</p></p>
