<p><strong>The Horizontal Electrophoresis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Agarose Gel Electrophoresis System</li><li>Polyacrylamide Gel Electrophoresis System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal electrophoresis systems market primarily includes two types: agarose gel electrophoresis systems and polyacrylamide gel electrophoresis systems. Agarose gel electrophoresis is commonly used for the separation of larger DNA fragments and nucleic acids, offering high resolution and ease of use. In contrast, polyacrylamide gel electrophoresis is more suited for separating smaller proteins and nucleic acids, providing higher resolution and versatility in analyzing complex mixtures. Both systems are essential tools in molecular biology and biochemistry laboratories.</p></p>
