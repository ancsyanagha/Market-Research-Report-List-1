<p><p>The ceramic packaging substrate material market comprises three key types: alumina, aluminum nitride (AlN), and silicon nitride. Alumina substrates are widely used for their electrical insulation and thermal conductivity properties, making them cost-effective for various applications. AlN substrates offer superior thermal conductivity and high temperature resistance, ideal for power electronics. Silicon nitride substrates are known for their mechanical strength and reliability under thermal stress, suitable for high-performance applications. Each type caters to different demands in electronics packaging and thermal management.</p></p>
