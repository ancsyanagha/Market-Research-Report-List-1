<p><p>The Foot Sensor Switch market includes various types, notably Infrared Photoelectric Sensors and Microwave Sensors. Infrared Photoelectric Sensors use light to detect foot presence, activating switches without physical contact, ideal for hygiene-focused environments. Microwave Sensors emit microwave signals that bounce off objects and detect foot movements, offering greater range and versatility, even through obstacles. Both sensor types enhance automation and safety in applications like industrial settings, healthcare facilities, and smart buildings, emphasizing convenience and user-friendly interaction.</p></p>
