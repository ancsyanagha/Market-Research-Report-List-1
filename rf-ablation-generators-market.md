<p><strong>The RF Ablation Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Fashion</li><li>Multi-polar Fashion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF ablation generators are categorized into monopolar and multipolar systems based on their configuration and application. Monopolar systems utilize a single electrode for treatment, effectively delivering radiofrequency energy to a targeted area while grounding through a separate pad, making them suitable for large tissue ablation. In contrast, multipolar systems employ multiple electrodes, allowing for more precise targeting and treatment of smaller areas with less thermal spread. This differentiation enhances the versatility and efficacy of RF ablation in various medical procedures.</p></p>
