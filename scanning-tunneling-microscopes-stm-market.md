The market for STMs includes two primary modes: Constant-height mode, where the tip maintains a fixed distance from the surface, enabling rapid scanning with reduced data acquisition time, ideal for measuring surface topography; and Constant-current mode, where the tip adjusts its height to maintain a stable tunneling current, providing more precise images, especially useful for analyzing surface properties and electronic structures.</p></p>
