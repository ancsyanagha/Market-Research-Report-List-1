<p><p>Polystyrene capacitors are widely used in electronic applications due to their stability and low dielectric absorption. The market is primarily segmented into axial and radial types. Axial polystyrene capacitors feature leads extending from both ends, making them suitable for through-hole mounting in tight spaces. Radial polystyrene capacitors have leads coming from one end, allowing for more compact designs and easier layout on PCBs. Both types are valued for their high precision and reliability in various circuits.</p></p>
