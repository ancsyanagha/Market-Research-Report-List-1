<p><p>The electric two-wheeler sharing market can be categorized into two main types: dockless and dock-based. Dockless systems allow users to pick up and drop off e-scooters or e-bikes at any designated location within a service area, providing flexibility and convenience. In contrast, dock-based systems require users to rent vehicles from and return them to specific docking stations, enhancing security and organization but potentially limiting accessibility. Both models aim to promote sustainable urban mobility and reduce traffic congestion.</p></p>
