<p><p>The cable fault detectors and locators market encompasses two main types: cable fault detectors and cable fault locators. Cable fault detectors identify the presence of faults in electrical cables, typically using techniques like time-domain reflectometry. On the other hand, cable fault locators provide precise geographic locations of these faults, enhancing maintenance efficiency. These devices are crucial for ensuring reliable electrical infrastructure, reducing downtime, and minimizing repair costs by swiftly pinpointing issues in extensive cable networks.</p></p>
