Grade 70 or Transport chains are designed for heavy-duty applications such as securing loads during transport, offering superior strength and durability. BBB Grade chains, also known as triple B grade chains, are characterized by their enhanced strength and are suitable for high-demand applications. Each type serves specific industry needs based on performance requirements.</p></p>
