<p><p>The Cardiac Cryoablation System market is primarily segmented into three types based on the cryogenic agents used: Argon Gas, Helium Gas, and Liquid Nitrogen. Argon and Helium gas systems utilize gas for cryoablation, allowing for precise temperature control during cardiac tissue ablation procedures. Liquid Nitrogen systems employ rapidly cooled nitrogen to achieve similar effects. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of efficacy, safety, and ease of use, catering to different clinical needs in treating cardiac arrhythmias.</p></p>
