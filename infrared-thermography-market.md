<p><p>The Infrared Thermography market comprises various technologies, including Laser and Structured Light systems. Laser thermography utilizes concentrated laser beams to detect temperature variations on surfaces, providing high-resolution thermal images for precise analysis. In contrast, Structured Light thermography projects a specific pattern onto a surface, mapping deformation and thermal distribution effectively. Both technologies cater to diverse industrial applications, including maintenance, quality control, and non-destructive testing, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in monitoring temperature-related phenomena across various sectors.</p></p>
