<p><strong>The LEO Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LEO antenna market can be categorized into two main types: active and passive. Active LEO antennas incorporate electronic components, enabling functionalities like beamforming and signal amplification, which enhance communication performance and adaptability to varying conditions. In contrast, passive LEO antennas rely on their physical structure to receive and transmit signals, offering a simpler design with lower costs but limited flexibility and performance. Both types cater to different applications and user needs within satellite communication frameworks.</p></p>
