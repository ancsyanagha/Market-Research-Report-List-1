<p><p>The Semiconductor Assembly and Test Equipment market comprises various types essential for chip production and testing. Wafer probe stations assess electrical properties of wafers before dicing. Die bonders attach semiconductor chips to substrates, ensuring precise placement. Dicing machines cut wafers into individual dies for packaging. Test handlers automate the testing of packaged semiconductors for performance and reliability. Sorters categorize tested chips based on specifications, while "others" includes ancillary equipment vital for enhanced assembly and testing processes.</p></p>
