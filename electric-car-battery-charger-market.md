<p><p>The electric car battery charger market is categorized into two main types: on-board and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are integrated within the vehicle, converting AC power from standard outlets into DC power for the battery, allowing for convenient home charging. Off-board chargers, typically found in public charging stations, provide higher power levels and faster charging solutions, often using dedicated equipment. Both types play crucial roles in supporting the growing demand for electric vehicle infrastructure and enhancing charging efficiency.</p></p>
