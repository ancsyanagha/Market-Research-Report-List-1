<p><p>The Variable Air Volume (VAV) system market consists of various types, primarily including single-duct, dual-duct, and fan-powered systems. Single-duct systems deliver conditioned air to multiple zones using dampers for temperature control. Dual-duct systems provide both hot and cold air, mixing them to achieve desired temperatures in different zones. Fan-powered VAV systems incorporate an auxiliary fan to enhance airflow, offering increased flexibility and efficiency. Additional variations, categorized as "others," may include specialized systems tailored for specific applications or environments.</p></p>
