<p><ul><li>Gas Engine Heat Pumps (GEnHP)</li><li>Gas Absorption Heat Pumps (GAbHP)</li><li>Gas Adsorption Heat Pumps (GAdHP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas heat pumps market comprises three main types: Gas Engine Heat Pumps (GEnHP), which utilize an internal combustion engine to drive the heat transfer process; Gas Absorption Heat Pumps (GAbHP), which rely on a heat-driven absorption cycle using refrigerants; and Gas Adsorption Heat Pumps (GAdHP), which employ solid adsorbents to capture and release heat. Each type offers distinct advantages in efficiency, flexibility, and environmental impact, catering to different heating and cooling applications across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
