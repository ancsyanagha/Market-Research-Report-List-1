<p><ul><li>HD Graphics</li><li>Integrated Graphics</li><li>Discrete Graphics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphics card market consists of three main types: Integrated Graphics, Discrete Graphics, and HD Graphics. Integrated Graphics are built into the CPU, offering basic performance for everyday tasks and casual gaming without the need for a separate GPU. Discrete Graphics, on the other hand, are standalone cards that provide superior performance for gaming, 3D rendering, and professional applications. HD Graphics typically refer to advanced integrated solutions that support high-definition video playback and enhanced graphical capabilities within laptops or lower-end desktops.</p></p>
