<p><strong>The Steel Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Left Regular Lay</li><li>Left Lang Lay</li><li>Right Regular Lay</li><li>Right Lang Lay</li><li>Alternate Lay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel cable market features various lay types that cater to specific applications. Left Regular Lay involves strands laid to the left, offering good flexibility; Left Lang Lay has strands laid more loosely, enhancing strength for high-stress environments. Right Regular Lay operates similarly but to the right, balancing flexibility and strength. Right Lang Lay provides superior strength and minimal torsion. Alternate Lay, combining both lay types, allows for flexibility and stability, making it suitable for diverse industrial uses. Each type meets distinct performance standards.</p></p>
