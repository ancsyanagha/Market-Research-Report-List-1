Mobile Headsets leverage smartphones for processing and display, offering portability and affordability. External Headsets connect to high-performance PCs, delivering superior graphics and immersive experiences for gaming and simulations. All-in-One Headsets integrate all necessary hardware within the headset itself, providing a standalone solution without external devices, making them user-friendly and convenient for various applications, including gaming, education, and entertainment.</p></p>
