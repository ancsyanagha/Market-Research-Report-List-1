<p><p>The vacuum blower market is categorized into four types based on vacuum levels. Low vacuum systems are used for general applications, achieving vacuum levels just below atmospheric pressure. Medium vacuum systems are suitable for processes needing deeper vacuum conditions. High vacuum systems are employed in specialized industrial and scientific applications, reaching significantly lower pressures. Ultra-high vacuum systems operate at extremely low pressures, essential for advanced research and technologies, such as semiconductor fabrication and surface science experiments. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
