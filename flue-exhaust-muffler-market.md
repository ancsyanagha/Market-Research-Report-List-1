<p><p>The flue exhaust muffler market is segmented into two main types: hot flue exhaust mufflers and cold flue exhaust mufflers. Hot flue exhaust mufflers are designed to manage high-temperature exhaust gases, typically found in industrial applications or heavy-duty engines, reducing noise and optimizing performance. In contrast, cold flue exhaust mufflers handle cooler exhaust gases, often used in residential heating systems or lighter machinery, focusing on efficiency and noise reduction while maintaining lower operational temperatures. Both types enhance safety and compliance in their respective applications.</p></p>
