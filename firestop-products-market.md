<p><p>The firestop products market consists of various types designed to prevent the spread of fire and smoke in buildings. Firestop blocks are dense materials used in wall systems to seal openings. Firestop mineral wool provides insulation and fire resistance in gaps. Firestop sealants are flexible compounds that fill joints and penetrations to maintain fire integrity. Firestop sheets are applied in larger areas to create barriers. Other products include coatings and intumescent materials that enhance fire resistance in diverse applications.</p></p>
