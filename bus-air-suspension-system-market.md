<p><p>The bus air suspension system market includes two main types: manual air suspension and electronic air suspension. Manual air suspension relies on adjustable air pressure levels to control ride height and comfort, allowing for basic customization by the user. In contrast, electronic air suspension utilizes advanced sensors and control systems to automatically adjust air pressure in real-time, optimizing ride quality, stability, and load distribution. Both systems enhance passenger comfort and vehicle performance, addressing the varying demands of modern bus transportation.</p></p>
