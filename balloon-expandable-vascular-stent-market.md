<p><p>Balloon-expandable vascular stents are medical devices implanted in blood vessels to treat conditions such as atherosclerosis and obstructive lesions. These stents are designed to improve blood flow by providing structural support to the vessel walls after a balloon catheter expands them during the procedure. Their ability to be deployed precisely and the minimal invasiveness of the procedure contribute to their growing popularity in interventional cardiology and peripheral vascular applications.</p><p>The Balloon-expandable Vascular Stent Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.4% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by several key factors, including the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, increased awareness of minimally invasive treatment options, and advancements in stent technology. Emerging trends include the development of drug-eluting balloon-expandable stents that release medication to reduce restenosis risk, and the integration of biodegradable materials for enhanced biocompatibility. Additionally, an increasing number of regulatory approvals and investments in research and development are propelling market growth. The expanding geriatric population and innovations in healthcare infrastructure further reflect the bright prospects for the balloon-expandable vascular stent market in the coming years.</p></p>
