<p><p>The carbon brakes market primarily consists of two types: carbon-ceramic composite materials and carbon-carbon composite materials. Carbon-ceramic composites are a blend of carbon fibers and ceramic matrices, offering excellent heat resistance and lightweight properties, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. In contrast, carbon-carbon composites consist of carbon fibers embedded in a carbon matrix, providing superior strength and thermal stability, often used in aerospace and motorsport applications. Both types enhance braking performance while reducing weight and wear.</p></p>
