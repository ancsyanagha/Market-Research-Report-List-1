<p><ul><li>Closed Type</li><li>Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical Restricted Access Barrier Systems (RABS) are designed to minimize contamination during drug manufacturing. The market comprises two primary types: closed type and open type. Closed type RABS maintains a sealed environment, using glove ports for operator access, thus offering a high level of protection against airborne contaminants. In contrast, open type RABS allows for easier operator access through flexible barriers, providing a balance between contamination control and operational efficiency. Both types enhance product safety and compliance in pharmaceutical processes.</p></p>
