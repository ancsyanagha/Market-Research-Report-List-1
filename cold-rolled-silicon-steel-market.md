<p><p>The Cold Rolled Silicon Steel market is categorized into two main types based on thickness. Cold Rolled Coils (Thickness ≥3mm) are typically used in applications requiring higher durability and strength, such as electrical transformers and motors. In contrast, Cold Rolled Coils (Thickness below 3mm) are more suited for lightweight applications, including electrical appliances and electronic components. Both categories offer unique properties, with the higher thickness providing enhanced magnetic performance, while thinner coils enable flexibility and intricate designs in manufacturing.</p></p>
