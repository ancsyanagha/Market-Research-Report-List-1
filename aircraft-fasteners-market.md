<p><p>The aircraft fasteners market is categorized into threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts and screws, offer secure fastening through interlocking threads, enabling easy assembly and disassembly. They are commonly used in critical applications requiring precise adjustments. Non-threaded fasteners, including rivets and pins, provide permanent or semi-permanent connections, ensuring structural integrity in airframes and components. Both types are essential for ensuring the safety and reliability of aircraft, catering to various installation and maintenance needs.</p></p>
