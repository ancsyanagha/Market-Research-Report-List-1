<p><p>Composite panels are engineered materials made from two or more constituent materials that provide a combination of desired properties. Typically, they consist of a core material sandwiched between two layers of a different material, resulting in a structure that is both lightweight and strong. These panels are widely used in construction, automotive, aerospace, and furniture applications due to their durability, thermal insulation, and aesthetic appeal.</p><p>The composite panel market is projected to experience significant growth, with an expected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 11.7% during the forecast period.
