<p><p>The Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) thermistor market is primarily divided into two types: Ceramic PTC and Polymer PTC. Ceramic PTC thermistors are made from ceramic materials and exhibit rapid resistance changes with temperature, making them ideal for overcurrent protection and self-regulating heating applications. Polymer PTC thermistors, on the other hand, utilize conductive polymer materials that provide advantages in flexibility and lower costs, suited for applications like circuit protection and temperature sensing. Both types serve critical roles in various electronic devices.</p></p>
