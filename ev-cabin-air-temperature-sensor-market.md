<p><strong>The EV Cabin Air Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermistor Type</li><li>Thermocouple Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EV cabin air temperature sensor market is segmented into three main types: thermistor type, thermocouple type, and others. </p><p>Thermistor type sensors utilize the resistance change in materials with temperature variations, offering rapid response and accuracy, ideal for cabin climate control. Thermocouple type sensors, in contrast, use the voltage generated between two different metals to measure temperature, suited for high-temperature environments but less common in cabins. The "others" category includes various emerging technologies and alternative sensors that cater to specific applications or technologies.</p></p>
