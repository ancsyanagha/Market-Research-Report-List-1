<p><strong>The Aerial Refueling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Probe and Drogue</li><li>Boom and Receptacle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerial refueling systems market primarily comprises two types: Probe and Drogue, and Boom and Receptacle. The Probe and Drogue system features a flexible hose with a drogue, which aircraft can connect to via a probe, allowing for fluid transfer during flight. In contrast, the Boom and Receptacle method involves a rigid boom extended from a tanker aircraft, which connects to a receptacle on the receiving aircraft for a faster, more efficient refueling process, often used in military operations.</p></p>
