<p><strong>The Gastroscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Gastroscope</li><li>Fiberoptic Gastroscope</li><li>Capsule Gastroscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gastroscopy market comprises three primary types: electronic gastroscopes, fiberoptic gastroscopes, and capsule gastroscopes. Electronic gastroscopes utilize advanced imaging technology to provide high-resolution visuals and often feature enhanced functionalities for diagnostics. Fiberoptic gastroscopes, being traditional, use flexible fiberoptic cables to enable visualization of the gastrointestinal tract, offering good image quality but with limited additional features. Capsule gastroscopes represent an innovative approach, allowing patients to swallow a small, camera-equipped capsule that captures images throughout the digestive system, facilitating non-invasive examinations.</p></p>
