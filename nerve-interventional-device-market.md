<p><p>The nerve interventional device market includes several types of tools used for procedures involving nerve tissues. Microcatheters are flexible tubes for precise delivery of treatments. Spring coils are used for embolization, blocking blood flow to specific areas. Brackets assist in stabilizing structures during interventions, while bolt removal bracket systems facilitate the extraction of fasteners in surgeries. Distal access catheters are designed for reaching remote vascular territories. The "other" category encompasses various miscellaneous devices utilized in nerve interventions.</p></p>
