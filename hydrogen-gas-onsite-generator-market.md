<p><p>The hydrogen gas onsite generator market features two primary types: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline solution for electrolysis, offering cost-effectiveness and durability, suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers employ a solid polymer membrane, providing higher efficiency and faster response times, making them ideal for dynamic applications. Both technologies contribute to the growing demand for renewable hydrogen production, supporting various industries and decarbonization efforts.</p></p>
