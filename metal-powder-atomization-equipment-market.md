<p><p>The metal powder atomization equipment market comprises three main types: gas atomization equipment, water atomization equipment, and other methods. Gas atomization involves leveraging high-velocity gas jets to rapidly cool and solidify molten metal, producing fine powders with uniform particle size. Water atomization employs high-pressure water streams to achieve similar results but is effective for larger volumes. Other equipment types include plasma and centrifugal atomization, each offering distinct benefits for specific applications in industries like aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
